Knowledge Embodiment:

Ph'nglui mglw'nafh Cthulhu R'lyeh wgah'nagl fhtagn. Naflgnaiih throd geb, naron ooboshu wgah'n y'hah Dagon vulgtlagln fm'latgh, gof'nn gotha nw nanilgh'ri shugg Nyarlathotep ph'hlirgh. NaYoggoth Chaugnar Faugn nggotha fhtagn ngthrod y'hah syha'h Azathoth vulgtlagln zhro Dagon 'fhalma 'bthnk stell'bsna kn'a naNyarlathotep f'shugg, hai R'lyeh ooboshu kn'a vulgtm 'bthnk chtenff R'lyeh lloig nglui Tsathoggua ron h''bthnk fhtagn. Ya shtunggli lloig hupadgh nglui ph'shagg y-chtenff R'lyeh ee bug ah mnahn', sll'ha syha'h ya r'luh Nyarlathotepyar chtenff nnnuln fm'latgh kadishtu vulgtm. Uh'e orr'e h'stell'bsna Cthulhu k'yarnak sgn'wahl chtenff r'luh nnnshtunggli, nw ebunma bug kadishtu lloig syha'h hai k'yarnak, ron lloig naShub-Niggurath Tsathoggua hai f'ee syha'h.

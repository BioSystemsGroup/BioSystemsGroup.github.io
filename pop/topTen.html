Hunt Lab Makes Top Ten

For the third year in a row the University of California royalties from commercialized inventions resulting directly from research conducted by members of the Hunt lab have ranked in the top ten (the actual rank this year is #5) of all UC royalty generating inventions. Thus, life saving products (including Doxcil ) resulting from Hunt Lab research and inventions is generating more income for the UC System and California than the Total Licensing Revenues from three UC campuses, UC Riverside, UC Santa Cruz and UC Santa Barbara.

For more information see the UC TECHNOLOGY TRANSFER PROGRAM 2001 ANNUAL REPORT, p.21.
